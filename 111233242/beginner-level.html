Beginner Level

Start your musical journey with a solid foundation in music

Course Overview

Our Beginner Level course is designed for absolute beginners who want to start their musical journey. Whether you're interested in guitar, piano, vocals, or any other instrument, this foundational course will help you build the essential skills needed for your musical future.

Duration:

12 Weeks (1 session per week, 60 minutes each)

Class Type:

One-on-one personalized sessions

Skill Level:

No prior experience needed

Instruments:

Guitar, Piano, Vocals, or other instruments of your choice

Locations:

Online, CBD Belapur, or Vashi

Anurag Naik

Lead Music Instructor

Professional musician with over 10 years of teaching experience

Course Curriculum

Week 1-2: Music Fundamentals

  • Introduction to your chosen instrument (parts, care, maintenance)
  • Proper posture and playing position
  • Basic finger exercises and coordination
  • Introduction to music notation and reading
  • First simple notes and sounds on your instrument

Week 3-4: Basic Theory and First Songs

  • Understanding rhythm and timing
  • Introduction to basic musical theory
  • Learning simple melodies
  • Playing your first easy songs
  • Beginning ear training exercises

Week 5-6: Expanding Skills

  • Additional techniques specific to your instrument
  • Building your musical vocabulary
  • Introduction to scales and patterns
  • Playing more complex songs
  • Beginning improvisational concepts

Week 7-8: Developing Musicality

  • Expression and dynamics in playing
  • Understanding musical structure
  • Building repertoire of songs
  • Practice techniques and routines
  • Overcoming common beginner challenges

Week 9-10: Building Confidence

  • Performance preparation fundamentals
  • Playing with backing tracks
  • Recording your progress
  • Troubleshooting common issues
  • Group playing introduction (optional)

Week 11-12: Final Project and Next Steps

  • Preparing final performance pieces
  • Consolidating learned skills
  • Performance for friends/family (optional)
  • Evaluating progress and achievements
  • Planning your continued musical journey
