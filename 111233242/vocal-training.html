Vocal Training

Discover your true voice and express yourself through singing

Course Overview

Our Vocal Training course is designed for students who want to develop their singing voice. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, our personalized one-on-one sessions will help you build a solid foundation in vocal technique, breath control, and performance skills.

Duration:

8 Weeks (1 session per week, 60 minutes each)

Class Type:

One-on-one personalized sessions

Skill Level:

Beginner to Intermediate

Styles Covered:

Contemporary, Pop, Classical, Folk (based on student preference)

Locations:

Online, CBD Belapur, or Vashi

Course Curriculum

Week 1-2: Foundation and Breath Control

  • Vocal anatomy and understanding your instrument
  • Proper posture for optimal vocal production
  • Diaphragmatic breathing techniques
  • Basic warm-up exercises
  • Assessment of vocal range and voice type

Week 3-4: Pitch and Resonance

  • Pitch matching exercises
  • Understanding vocal resonance
  • Head and chest voice coordination
  • Ear training for vocalists
  • Beginning simple songs appropriate for your range

Week 5-6: Vocal Technique Development

  • Expanding vocal range safely
  • Vocal dynamics (soft to loud singing)
  • Articulation and diction
  • Blending vocal registers
  • Working on songs of personal choice

Week 7-8: Performance Skills

  • Microphone technique
  • Stage presence and performance anxiety management
  • Emotional connection to music
  • Preparing for a recording or performance
  • Building a sustainable practice routine

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have any previous singing experience?

No previous experience is required. Our vocal training program is designed for beginners and we'll tailor the lessons to your current skill level.

What if I'm tone deaf? Can I still learn to sing?

True tone deafness is extremely rare. Most people who think they are "tone deaf" simply haven't developed their ear yet. Our ear training exercises can help you improve your pitch recognition and singing.

What style of music will I learn to sing?

The course covers foundational techniques applicable to all styles. You can focus on your preferred style (pop, rock, classical, etc.), and your instructor will guide you accordingly.

Will I be able to perform at the end of the course?

Yes! At the end of the 8-week program, students have the option to participate in a showcase performance to apply what they've learned in a supportive environment.
