Intermediate Level

Take your musical skills to the next level with advanced techniques and deeper understanding

Course Overview

Our Intermediate Level course is designed for musicians who have already mastered the basics and are ready to advance their skills. This program focuses on developing more complex techniques, deeper musical theory understanding, and enhanced performance capabilities.

Duration:

10 Weeks (1 session per week, 90 minutes each)

Class Type:

Small group classes (4-6 students)

Skill Level:

Prior experience required (at least 6-12 months of consistent practice)

Instruments:

Guitar, Piano, Vocals, or continue with your primary instrument

Locations:

Online, CBD Belapur, or Vashi

Priya Sharma

Senior Music Instructor

Experienced performer with expertise in multiple instruments and advanced theory

Course Curriculum

  • Instrument-specific advanced techniques
  • Developing speed, accuracy, and dexterity
  • Nuanced tone production and control
  • Advanced music notation and sight-reading
  • Technical exercises for skill enhancement

  • Advanced scales and modes
  • Complex chord structures and progressions
  • Key modulations and transitions
  • Harmonic analysis of music pieces
  • Form and structure in musical composition

  • Exploration of various musical genres
  • Style-specific techniques and approaches
  • Ornamentation and expressive devices
  • Authentic interpretation of different music styles
  • Developing your unique musical voice

  • Playing/performing with others
  • Rhythm section techniques
  • Accompaniment approaches
  • Collaborative improvisation
  • Group dynamics and communication

  • Intermediate performance techniques
  • Preparing a diverse repertoire
  • Managing performance anxiety
  • Introduction to basic recording techniques
  • Culminating performance preparation

Collaborative Learning

Develop ensemble skills by working with other musicians at your level in small group settings.

Performance Opportunities

Participate in student showcases and performances to gain valuable stage experience.

Recording Session

Experience a basic recording session to learn about the studio environment and techniques.
