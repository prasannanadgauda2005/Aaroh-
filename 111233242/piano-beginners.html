Piano for Beginners

Start your journey into the beautiful world of piano playing

Course Overview

Our Piano for Beginners course is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of piano playing in a supportive group environment. This course covers basic techniques, essential music theory, and simple pieces to build your confidence and skills from the ground up.

Duration:

10 Weeks (1 session per week, 90 minutes each)

Class Type:

Small group classes (4-6 students)

Skill Level:

No prior experience needed

Instruments:

Digital pianos provided during in-person classes

Locations:

Online, CBD Belapur, or Vashi

Priya Sharma

Piano Instructor

Classically trained pianist with over 8 years of teaching experience

Course Curriculum

Week 1-2: Introduction to Piano

  • Understanding the piano keyboard layout
  • Proper sitting posture and hand position
  • Basic finger exercises
  • Introduction to music notation
  • Playing simple 5-finger patterns

Week 3-4: Reading Music and Basic Theory

  • Reading notes on the treble and bass clefs
  • Understanding rhythm and time signatures
  • Basic music theory concepts
  • Introduction to major scales (C, G major)
  • Simple two-hand coordination exercises

Week 5-6: Chords and Simple Songs

  • Introduction to basic chords
  • Playing melodies with simple left-hand accompaniment
  • Learning easy, recognizable songs
  • Developing dynamic control (soft vs. loud playing)
  • Introduction to pedaling techniques

Week 7-8: More Complex Pieces

  • Introduction to minor scales
  • Playing pieces with more complex rhythms
  • Developing independence between hands
  • Playing duets with other students
  • Sight-reading exercises

Week 9-10: Performance Preparation

  • Polishing selected pieces for performance
  • Addressing technical challenges
  • Performance techniques and managing nerves
  • Mini-recital with peers
  • Next steps in your piano journey

Learning Materials
