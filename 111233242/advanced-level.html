Advanced Level

Master professional performance techniques and develop your unique musical identity

Course Overview

Our Advanced Level course is designed for serious musicians ready to take their craft to a professional level. This intensive program focuses on mastery of complex techniques, sophisticated musical expression, performance excellence, and developing your unique artistic voice.

Duration:

8 Weeks (1 session per week, 60 minutes each)

Class Type:

One-on-one personalized sessions

Skill Level:

Advanced (2+ years of dedicated practice and intermediate course completion)

Specialization:

Focus on your primary instrument with advanced repertoire and techniques

Locations:

Online, CBD Belapur, or Vashi

Anurag Naik

Master Instructor

Course Curriculum

Week 1-2: Virtuoso Techniques

  • Advanced instrument-specific techniques
  • Virtuosic passages and challenging repertoire
  • Technical mastery and problem-solving
  • Nuanced tone control and expression
  • Advanced practice methodologies

Week 3-4: Advanced Musicianship

  • Complex harmonic analysis and application
  • Advanced improvisational techniques
  • Sophisticated phrasing and interpretation
  • Complex rhythmic concepts
  • Advanced sight-reading and transcription

Week 5-6: Artistic Development

  • Establishing your unique musical identity
  • Composition and arrangement techniques
  • Advanced musical interpretation
  • Stylistic authenticity across genres
  • Creating original music/arrangements

Week 7-8: Professional Performance

  • Professional performance preparation
  • Advanced stage presence and audience engagement
  • Performance psychology and mindset
  • Recording techniques and studio readiness
  • Final showcase preparation and execution
